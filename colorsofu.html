Colors of You by Owen Herbst

Aurora borealis,
Flecks of radiation
Hurtling down blazing the atmosphere
Lightning hue

Describes you.
Gold, silver, and saffire blue
Ripping it across the sky.
Words cannot describe
The colors of you.

Flecks, and the night's bright,
By dazzling, northern light's sight
and the beauty.
Not of the radiation,
But the colors of you.

And from the North, absolute,
Cracked with lightning hue
A meteroric perspective
On the colors of you.
