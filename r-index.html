Welcome to Herbst Reflexion

Why We Believe in Herbst Reflexion

Like many others before us, we believe in living the best life possible. One aspect of life which we must all contend with is ourself. Our body and mind are integral to our happiness, and the mind is what Herbst Reflexion will address.

Self-improvement is key to actualizing the potential we all enter the world with. To Herbst Reflexion, art is a form of self improvement. As one of our contributors, Sara Herbst, puts it, " Everytime I leave an art museum, I feel like a better person."

Whether it's viewing and interacting with art or creating it, or reading poetry or writing your own, Herbst Reflexion would like to share art, philosophy, literature, and music with its viewers.

By publishing essays on current events, analyses of literature and art, creating and publishing our own works, Herbst Reflexion will bring the enrichment of art to viewers, whether they have the natural eye for art, or are trying to understand the complicated meanings behind the ideas they are fascinated by. Art is for everyone, just open your mind!
